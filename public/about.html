💼 Rozgar Sathi Kya Hai?

Rozgar Sathi ek free, one-stop platform hai jo India ke sarkari naukri ke aspirants ke liye banaya gaya hai. Yahan Railway, SSC, Banking, UPSC, BPSC aur State PSC ki vacancies ek jagah milti hain — poori eligibility, fees, important dates aur direct apply links ke saath.

Hamara goal hai ki ek aspirant ko kisi bhi sarkari naukri ke baare mein poori jaankari ek hi jagah mile — chahe woh eligibility ho, application fee ho, form kahan se bharna ho, ya exam date kya hai.

✨ Features

  • 📋 Sarkari vacancy listings with complete details
  • 💰 Fee structure for all categories — General, OBC, SC/ST, Female
  • 📅 Important dates — notification, apply start/end, exam, result
  • 🎯 Selection process step-by-step for each exam
  • 💵 Salary range clearly mentioned
  • 🔗 Direct apply link + official notification PDF link
  • 📄 Free Resume Builder with PDF download (watermarked)
  • 📊 Application Tracker — track all your applied jobs
  • 🔐 Google Sign-In — same account works on Samachar Sathi & Pariksha Sathi
  • 🌐 Hindi & English toggle
  • 📱 Fully mobile friendly
