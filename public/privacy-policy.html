🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026 | Effective Date: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rozgar Sathi ("we", "our", "the platform") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website at https://snakeeye-sudo.github.io/rozgar-sathi/. By using Rozgar Sathi, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google OAuth.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns (via Google Analytics, if enabled).
  • User-Generated Data: Resume data and application tracker entries you create — stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore if you are logged in.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Rozgar Sathi service
  • To enable cross-app Single Sign-On (SSO) across Samachar Sathi, Pariksha Sathi, and Rozgar Sathi
  • To sync your saved jobs and application tracker across devices (when logged in)
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To communicate important updates (only if you have opted in)

3. Google Sign-In & Firebase

We use Firebase Authentication (by Google) for sign-in. When you sign in with Google, your basic profile information (name, email, photo) is stored in Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID.

This data is used solely to provide personalized features like saved jobs and application tracking. We do not sell or share this data with third parties.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

Rozgar Sathi uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (authentication, preferences)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense for displaying relevant ads

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Resume data and tracker entries are stored in your browser's localStorage by default — no server involved.
  • Firebase Firestore: If logged in, your saved jobs and tracker data may be synced to Firebase (encrypted in transit via HTTPS).
  • No Passwords Stored: We use Google OAuth — we never store your password.
  • Data Retention: Your Firestore data is retained until you delete your account or request deletion.

6. Third-Party Links

Rozgar Sathi contains links to official government websites (ssc.gov.in, rrbapply.gov.in, ibps.in, etc.) and other third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Rozgar Sathi is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Export your data (resume, tracker entries) at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email provided on our Contact page.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Rozgar Sathi after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please visit our Contact page or reach out via GitHub: @SnakeEye-sudo.
